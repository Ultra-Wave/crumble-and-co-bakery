Our Story

A Kitchen Full of
Good Things

🌸

Crumble & Co. was born in 2011 from a farmhouse kitchen and a stubborn belief that bread should taste like bread — made slowly, with real ingredients, and baked until it smells like Sunday morning.

Our founder, Mae Holloway, learned to bake from her grandmother and spent years perfecting sourdough starters, laminated doughs, and the perfectly sticky cinnamon roll before opening our first little shop.

Today we bake over 40 varieties of bread, pastry, and cake every single morning — all from scratch, all with love, and all available for pickup or local delivery.
